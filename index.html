About Me

I'm Randika Navagamuwa

rnavagamuwa is a Solutions Architect at StarTree. StarTree was founded by the original developers of Apache Pinot and provides a fully managed platform for Apache Pinot. Apache Pinot is a realtime distributed OLAP designed for low-latency, high throughput query execution.

Before he joined StarTree, Randika worked as a Solutions Architect at WSO2. WSO2 is the world’s #1 open-source integration vendor, helping digitally driven organizations become integration agile. The company’s hybrid platform for developing, reusing, running, and managing integrations prevents lock-in through open-source software that runs on-premises or in the cloud. At WSO2, Randika lead the Solutions Architecture and Sales effort in the South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation (SAARC) sub-region. He managed to grow the Indian sub-region to 25 + customers from less than 5 during his period.

Before WSO2, Randika worked as a Senior Software Engineer at Adroitlogic Lanka (Pvt) Ltd. He was responsible for Adroitlogic AS2 Gateway and maintained several customer projects.

Randika has won the “Fritz Kunz Memorial Trophy” award for the most outstanding student in 2012 at the annual prize-giving of Ananda College, Colombo. He holds a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka.

He is a strong believer of open source world of software development, and has successfully completed the Google Summer of Code Program in 2016 with Eclipse Foundation, and has contributed to a number of open source projects since then. Randika spends his leisure time following sports and watching TV series.
